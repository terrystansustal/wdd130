Overview

Purpose

The purpose and goal of this website is to build revenue for the bus company by advertising the website on social media, partners, and passengers. Furthermore, the website will give information about the company's story, schedule, contact information, bus rentals, and location. This will give the bus company an advantage against other competitors because the passengers will see that the website is trustable and professional.

Audience

The target audience of this website are every citizen of the community, especially those who are disabled that needs public transportation. Financial wise, the target passengers are anyone because the bus fare will be affordable. The passengers will want a clean and comfortable bus as they go to their destination. The goal is to always meet the passengers' needs and wants by cleaning the bus every day. Lastly, passengers of the community can access this website on their desktop, laptop, tablet, and smartphone.

Content

Home page

Many people around the world rely on public transportation in order to get to work, school, to visit a friend, and so on. In addition, many organizations look for bus companies to hire for their business trips and so on. People will come to visit the website to look for a trusted bus company that they can rely on for public transportation and business trips. The questions that the visitors' will expect to be answered from the website are the bus fare, bus rental rates, the route, and contact information. The activities that the visitor will be able to do on the website are to learn more about the bus company and inquire for bus rates, schedule, and bus rentals. The purpose of this home-page is to quickly introduce the company with a styled lay-out that's appealing to the visitors' eyes, provide specific updates about the company, have a map for the bus route, and to welcome the visitor to the website. A hero image will be provided to welcome the visitors to the home-page. In addition, there will be navigation links that will connect the visitors to other pages of the website. Furthermore, the home-page will provide a map of the bus route for a quick reference of the passengers about the start and end point of the bus routes. Lastly, the home-page of the website will provide at least 3 detailed paragraphs such as featured stories, services, and other company's movements. This will encourage the visitors to view the website because it offers specific and detailed information about possible inquiries of the passengers.

About Us

To give a glimpse of who the company is and how they started, the second page of the website will give a brief story about the company. Learning about how a company started is very interesting to many people. Sometimes people like to read articles or quick summaries on how the company was established. The second page of the website will be about how the company was started from scratch. The content of this website will have pictures of the early days of the company. It will feature old models of the bus that the company started with and their recent and modern models. In addition, a text paragraph will be included to provide a story about the early days of the company, to explain the pictures, and how successful it became. The second page of this website will be very important because it will show the visitors the diligence that helped the bus company to reach where they are now.

Images for the Page 2

Rates

After setting-up expectations with the visitors, the purpose of the third page of this website is to provide more detailed information about the company's services, bus fares, bus routes, contact information, bus schedule, and other inquiries on renting a bus. The website will provide a breakdown of areas where the bus will pass by with corresponding bus fares on each of the areas. Likewise, the website will also update the bus schedule from the start point to end point. Furthermore, the second page will provide contact information for those who are planning to rent the bus for their trips with their business, organization, church, and etc. The contact information will be a phone number and an email address. The visitor will be able to choose which method to contact the bus company. Lastly, a bus schedule will be provided per stop and this will help the visitor because he or she will know when to go to the bus stop. It will also help the visitor not to miss the bus. The content for the second page will answer the questions that most passengers have.
