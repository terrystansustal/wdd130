About Stan Liner

Goals & Vision

Stan Liner has been a trusted company for passengers and business owners. Our vision is to satisfy and meet the clients' needs. Our goal to accomplish our vision is to provide great customer service by responding to inquiries in less than 24 hours, assisting the passengers, and having a friendly environment

Early days

Stan Liner was founded by Augusto T. Abella and Susan N. Sustal back in the 1980's. At first the company's name was called Jhepoy when the units were only Baby Buses. Augusto drove the baby buses during the early days of the company. The buses back then weren't air conditioned and very small. Approximately, the bus can only fit 20 passengers.

Story of Success

Throughout the years, Augusto and Susan worked really hard to maintain the growth of the company. Both of them were diligent. Eventually, they invested on buying new buses to expand their business. Augusto and Susan bought multiple buses that's airconditioned that can fit 60 passengers. These buses traveled from Cavite to Manila. In addition, other companies rented the bus for their trips. With countless of passengers and the growth of networking, Stan Liner became a well-known bus company in Cavite.
