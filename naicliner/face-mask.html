Face Mask Policy

In the first quarter of 2020, we required our passengers to wear a face mask for their own and other people's safety due to Covid-19. With the new restrictions of the government, we are pleased to announce that wearing a face mask is now voluntarily. We highly encourage those who has a cold/fever to wear a face mask to prevent from spreading a disease/virus. This new policy will be implemented on November 1, 2022.
